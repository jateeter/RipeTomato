## 👥 Who This Helps

### 🏠 For Clients (Individuals Experiencing Homelessness)

**What you can do:**
- 🔍 Find nearby meals, shelter, health services, and job training
- 📅 See weekly schedules and sign up for programs
- 🔒 Control who sees your personal information
- 📱 Access services even without internet (offline mode)
- 🌤️ Get weather alerts that affect services

**No registration required** - just tap "Client Portal" and start exploring!

### 👨‍💼 For Shelter & Services Staff

**What you can do:**
- ⚡ Update bed availability in real-time
- 📊 See who's enrolled in today's programs
- 🗺️ Find other facilities and their services
- ⚠️ Receive weather alerts affecting operations
- 📞 Contact clients (with their permission)

**Stay connected** with real-time updates and coordinated care.

### 📈 For Managers & Administrators

**What you can do:**
- 📊 View dashboards showing service usage and trends
- 🔄 Control when data refreshes from external sources
- 📈 Generate reports on program effectiveness
- ⚙️ Configure system settings and alerts
- 🗺️ Manage facility information across the network

**Make data-driven decisions** to improve services.

## 🚀 Quick Start Guides

### For Clients: Getting Started in 3 Steps

**Step 1: Access the Portal**
- Visit the web address (provided by staff)
- Tap the **"Client Portal 📱"** button
- No login required!

**Step 2: Explore Services**
- Browse the weekly schedule
- Tap on any program to see details
- Check locations and times

**Step 3: Enroll**
- Tap **"Enroll"** on activities you want to join
- See your schedule in the "Overview" tab
- Come back anytime to view or cancel

📱 **Tip**: Save the website to your home screen for quick access!

### For Staff: Daily Operations

**Morning Routine:**
1. Open Services Manager Dashboard
2. Update today's bed availability
3. Check weather alerts
4. Review enrolled participants

**Throughout the Day:**
- Mark beds as occupied/available
- Check client enrollments
- Update service status
- Coordinate with other facilities

**End of Day:**
- Final bed count update
- Review tomorrow's schedule
- Check alert notifications

### For Managers: Weekly Tasks

**Monday:**
- Review weekend reports
- Check facility capacity trends
- Refresh external data (HMIS, weather)

**Wednesday:**
- Mid-week analytics review
- Staff coordination meeting prep
- Service adjustment planning

**Friday:**
- Week-end summary reports
- Plan for weekend coverage
- Review upcoming week's schedule

## 🔐 Privacy & Security

### Your Data, Your Rules

**What We Protect:**
- ✅ Personal contact information
- ✅ Health and medical records
- ✅ Enrollment history
- ✅ Communication preferences

**How We Protect It:**
- 🔒 Encrypted storage
- 🔐 Secure connections (HTTPS)
- 👤 Client-controlled sharing
- 🚫 No selling or sharing with third parties
- ✅ HIPAA-aware design

###How Privacy Works

1. **You're in Control**
   - Decide what to share and with whom
   - Grant or revoke access anytime
   - See exactly who has access

2. **Granular Permissions**
   - Share email but not phone
   - Share location but not health info
   - Different permissions for different staff

3. **Transparent Access**
   - See who accessed your data
   - View what information was shared
   - Audit trail of all access

4. **Right to be Forgotten**
   - Request data deletion
   - Export your information
   - Transfer to other services
